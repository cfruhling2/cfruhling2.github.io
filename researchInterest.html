Hi, thanks for stopping by

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Purdue University working on topics such as nanolasing, nonlinear optics and time-varying media. I am, however, interested in all nonlinear optical phenomenon and studying the ultrafast interaction of light and matter. I am also interested in the developing field of quantum optics, especially with regard to optical computation and communication.
